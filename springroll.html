Vegetable Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  1. Spring roll wrappers
  2. Shredded cabbage
  3. Carrots
  4. Bean sprouts
  5. Soy sauce
  6. Sesame oil

Method:

  1. Fill spring roll wrappers with a mixture of shredded vegetables.
  2. Roll tightly and seal the edges. Fry or bake until golden brown.
  3. Serve with soy sauce for dipping.
